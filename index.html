About Us

The New York Giants are called the Giants for a reason, considering their franchise has been a giant success.

Believe it or not, the New York Giants are in New Jersey. Their stadium is Met Life stadium and it's located 5 miles west of New York City. They share the stadium with the New York Jets as well. The New York Giants were established in 1925. They originally played at the Polo Grounds in upper Manhattan for a few decades.
